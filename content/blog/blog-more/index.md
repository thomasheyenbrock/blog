It sounds so easy, but it sometimes is really hard: Writing.

I started doing it a while ago. I just wanted to share some of my knowledge
with my colleagues at work. So I used some of my time at work to write it down.
We were using Atlassians `Confluence` as our internal wiki, and it also offers
a blog section.
